Salon Policies

  1. 1. Appointments & Cancellations - Appointments must be booked in advance, with at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations to avoid fees.
  2. 2. Punctuality - Arrive on time; clients more than 15 minutes late may need to reschedule or pay a late fee.
  3. 3. Hair Preparation - Hair must be clean, detangled, and product-free, or a detangling fee may apply.
  4. 4. Guests & Children - Only clients receiving services are allowed; please arrange childcare in advance.
  5. 5. Payment & Deposits - A non-refundable deposit is required, with the balance due at the time of service.
  6. 6. Respect & Conduct - Disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated, and failure to follow policies may result in service refusal.
By booking with us, you agree to these policies. Thank you for your cooperation!
