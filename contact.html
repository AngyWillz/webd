Salon Policies

  • 1. Appointments & Cancellations - Appointments must be booked in advance, with at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations to avoid fees.
  • 2. Punctuality - Arrive on time; clients more than 15 minutes late may need to reschedule or pay a late fee.
  • 3. Hair Preparation - Hair must be clean, detangled, and product-free, or a detangling fee may apply.
  • 4. Guests & Children - Only clients receiving services are allowed; please arrange childcare in advance.
  • 5. Payment & Deposits - A non-refundable deposit is required, with the balance due at the time of service.
  • 6. Respect & Conduct - Disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated, and failure to follow policies may result in service refusal.
